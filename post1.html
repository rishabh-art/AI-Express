OTC - General Introduction

OTC Founding Perspective: OTC, abbreviation for Organisation of Terrorist(Terrorism) Council, is an organisation that believes in the power of terrorism to benefit the lives of many. While terrorism is currently used to threaten and usually degrades mankind's progress; we believe terrorism can also be used as a tool to maintain order, discipline and ensure progress by force and deterrence in the society. This will enable mankind to push beyond its limits than ever before. Further, terrorism can also be used as a tool to ensure the global powers are maintained under control so as to not threaten or have too much power which can be used against the benefits of mankind.
